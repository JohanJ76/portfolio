Hello and welcome!



My name is Johan, and I have the pleasure of working as a graphic designer for the past 22 years.

My journey in the world of design has been an incredible adventure, filled with creativity, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of aesthetic excellence. I specialize in designing for both white paper formats and digital media, seamlessly blending the two to create visually engaging and impactful designs.

My passion lies in modern, clean, and minimalistic styles, I am not limited to it!. I believe that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication, and I strive to reflect that philosophy in every project I undertake. Whether it's a corporate, small business or start-up white paper, an eye-catching digital advertisement, or a sleek logo design, I am committed to delivering designs that are not only visually pleasing but also functionally effective.

I have practical experience in various creative fields, including designing with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe After Effects, and CorelDRAW. You can count on me for services such as Branding Identity, Flyers, Brochures, Banners, Posters, Magazines, Catalogues, Annual Reports, and other Print and Digital Media designs.

Creativity doesn't adhere to a strict schedule for me. While the traditional 9 to 5 workday is productive, I often find my best ideas come to me in the stillness of early mornings or the quiet of late evenings. My creative process is a continuous cycle of inspiration, ideation, and execution, driven by an intrinsic love for design and artistic expression.

When I'm not immersed in design projects, I indulge in a few of my favorite pastimes. I'm a sci-fi movie enthusiast, always on the lookout for the next epic space opera or thought-provoking dystopian narrative. Board games are another passion of mine, offering a fun and strategic escape from the digital world. And, of course, coffee is my constant companion, fueling my creativity and keeping me energized throughout the day.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know a bit about me and my work. Feel free to reach out for more information or to discuss an upcoming project.

I am excited about the possibility of working with you.
